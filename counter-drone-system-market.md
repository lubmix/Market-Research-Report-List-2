<p><strong>The Counter Drone System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Detection Systems</li><li>Neutralizing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Detection systems in the counter drone system market are designed to identify and track unauthorized drones, providing early warning of potential threats. These systems utilize various technologies such as radar, acoustic sensors, and radio frequency detectors to detect drones in the surrounding airspace. Neutralizing systems, on the other hand, are designed to actively combat unauthorized drones by disrupting their control signals or even physically disabling them. These systems typically use technologies such as jamming devices or kinetic weapons to neutralize the threat posed by rogue drones.</p></p>
