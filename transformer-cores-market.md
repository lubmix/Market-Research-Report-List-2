<p><ul><li>Steel Laminated Cores</li><li>Solid Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transformer cores are crucial components in power distribution systems, enabling efficient energy transfer. Steel laminated cores, composed of thin layers of steel, offer reduced eddy current losses and improved efficiency. On the other hand, solid cores, made of a single block of magnetic material, provide higher magnetic permeability and lower core loss. The market for steel laminated cores is popular due to their cost-effectiveness and versatility, while solid cores are favored for applications requiring high efficiency and performance. Both types cater to the diverse needs of the transformer industry.</p></p>
