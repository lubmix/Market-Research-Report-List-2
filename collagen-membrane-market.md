<p><p>Collagen membrane market consists of different types such as cowhide and pigskin. Cowhide collagen membranes are derived from cow skin and are known for their high mechanical strength and flexibility, making them ideal for guided bone regeneration procedures. On the other hand, pigskin collagen membranes are derived from pig skin and are valued for their biocompatibility and ability to easily conform to the shape of the defect site. Both types of collagen membranes are widely used in dental and orthopedic surgeries for tissue regeneration and wound healing.</p></p>
