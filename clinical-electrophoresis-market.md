<p><ul><li>Slab Electrophoresis</li><li>Zone Electrophoresis</li><li>Paper Electrophoresis</li><li>Gel Electrophoresis</li><li>Isoelectric-Focusing</li><li>Immune-Electrophoresis</li><li>Capillary Electrophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clinical electrophoresis market involves different types such as slab electrophoresis, zone electrophoresis, paper electrophoresis, gel electrophoresis, isoelectric focusing, immune-electrophoresis, and capillary electrophoresis. Slab electrophoresis is used for separating proteins and nucleic acids, while zone electrophoresis is used for separating ions. Paper electrophoresis is a simple method for separating biological molecules. Gel electrophoresis is commonly used for DNA analysis. Isoelectric focusing separates proteins based on their isoelectric point. Immune-electrophoresis is used for analyzing antigen-antibody interactions. Capillary electrophoresis is a high-resolution technique for separating molecules.</p></p>
