<p><p>The IR Flame Detector market consists of three main types: Single IR, Dual IR, and Multi IR detectors. Single IR detectors are designed to detect flames based on infrared radiation from a single wavelength. Dual IR detectors use two wavelengths to improve accuracy and reduce false alarms. Multi IR detectors utilize multiple wavelengths for even more precise flame detection, making them ideal for environments where accuracy is crucial. Each type offers unique benefits and is suited for specific applications based on the level of precision required.</p></p>
