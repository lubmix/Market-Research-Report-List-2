<p><p>Printed Circuit Board (PCB) materials market is segmented into types such as Photoresist, Copper-Clad Laminate (CCL), Glass Fabric, and Others. Photoresist materials are used for masking and etching processes. CCL materials consist of copper foil bonded to a substrate for conducting electricity. Glass Fabric materials provide strength and rigidity to the PCB. Other materials in the market include solder mask materials, ink, and adhesives. Each type of material plays a crucial role in the manufacturing and functionality of PCBs.</p></p>
