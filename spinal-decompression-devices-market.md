<p><p>Spinal decompression devices can be classified into two main types: electric adjustment and manual adjustment. Electric adjustment devices utilize automated systems to apply traction to the spine, helping to relieve pressure and pain. On the other hand, manual adjustment devices require the user or a therapist to manually adjust the traction levels. Both types of devices are used to treat conditions such as herniated discs, sciatica, and spinal stenosis by stretching and realigning the spine to reduce pain and improve mobility.</p></p>
