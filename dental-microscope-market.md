<p><p>The dental microscope market includes two main types: portable and fixed microscopes. Fixed microscopes are permanently installed in a specific location in the office and are typically more advanced in terms of features and magnification capabilities. Both types offer dentists the ability to improve precision during procedures, enhance visualization, and provide better patient outcomes. The choice between portable and fixed microscopes depends on the specific needs and preferences of the dental practice.</p></p>
