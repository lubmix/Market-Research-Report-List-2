<p><strong>The UPS Battery Packs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Battery Packs</li><li>External Battery Packs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UPS battery packs come in two types: internal battery packs and external battery packs. Internal battery packs are integrated within the UPS unit itself, providing backup power in case of outages. External battery packs, on the other hand, are connected externally to the UPS unit to expand its battery capacity, offering longer runtime during power failures. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of users, providing flexibility and reliability in ensuring continuous power supply for critical equipment and devices.</p></p>
