<p><p>Digital-to-Analog Converters (DAC) are used to convert digital signals into analog signals. There are different types of DACs including R-2R DAC, which uses a resistor ladder network; String DAC, which utilizes a string of resistors; Current Source and Sink DAC, which generates output currents; and others such as delta-sigma DAC. Each type has its own advantages and applications based on factors like accuracy, speed, and cost. These DAC types cater to a wide range of industries including telecommunications, industrial automation, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
