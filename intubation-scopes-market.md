<p><p>Intubation scopes are medical devices used to assist in the insertion of an endotracheal tube into a patient's airway. There are two main types of intubation scopes: rigid and flexible. Rigid intubation scopes are straight and stiff, allowing for easier navigation through the airway. Flexible intubation scopes are more versatile in their movements, making them ideal for difficult airway situations. Both types of scopes play a crucial role in ensuring successful intubation procedures in medical settings.</p></p>
