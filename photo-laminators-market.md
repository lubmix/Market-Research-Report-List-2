<p><ul><li>Pouch Laminator</li><li>Roll Laminator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photo laminators are available in two main types - pouch laminators and roll laminators. Pouch laminators work by encapsulating documents in a plastic pouch that is then run through a heated machine to seal the edges. Roll laminators use a continuous roll of laminating film to cover larger documents or items. Pouch laminators are more commonly used for smaller documents like photos, while roll laminators are more suitable for larger items like posters or banners. Both types of laminators offer protection and durability to printed materials.</p></p>
