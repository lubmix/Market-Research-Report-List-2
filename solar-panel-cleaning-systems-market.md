<p><p>Solar panel cleaning systems are categorized into three main types - water based systems, electrostatic systems, and robotic systems. Water based systems use water to wash away dirt and debris from the panels. Electrostatic systems use charged water to attract dirt particles and remove them from the surface of the panels. Robotic systems are automated machines that physically scrub the panels clean. Each system has its own set of benefits and is used based on the specific requirements and preferences of the user.</p></p>
