<p><strong>The Discrete Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wirewound Coils</li><li>Deposited Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Discrete inductors are categorized into two main types: wirewound coils and deposited coils. Wirewound coils are made by winding wire around a core material, typically used for high current applications due to their robust construction. Deposited coils, on the other hand, are manufactured using thin film deposition techniques to create a compact and lightweight design, suitable for high-frequency applications. Both types cater to different market needs, with wirewound coils favored for their durability and deposited coils for their compact size and efficiency.</p></p>
