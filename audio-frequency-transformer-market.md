<p><strong>The Audio Frequency Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Input Transformer</li><li>Output Transformer</li><li>Interstage Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio Frequency Transformers are categorized into three main types based on their position and function in an electronic circuit: Input Transformer, Output Transformer, and Interstage Transformer. Input transformers are used to match the impedance of the input signal to the amplifier circuit. Output transformers are used to match the amplifier circuit's impedance to the load, such as speakers. Interstage transformers are used to couple stages of amplification together while maintaining signal integrity. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring proper signal transfer and amplification in audio systems.</p></p>
