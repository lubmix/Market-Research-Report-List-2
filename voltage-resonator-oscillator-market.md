<p><p>Voltage resonator oscillator is a type of electronic oscillator that generates an oscillating signal by using a resonant circuit. The three main types in the market are Wien Bridge Oscillator, RC Phase Shift Oscillator, and Hartley Oscillator. The Wien Bridge Oscillator uses a bridge circuit to generate a sinusoidal output, the RC Phase Shift Oscillator uses capacitors and resistors to generate phase shift, and the Hartley Oscillator uses inductor-capacitor tank circuit to produce oscillations. These oscillators are widely used in various electronic devices for signal generation.</p></p>
