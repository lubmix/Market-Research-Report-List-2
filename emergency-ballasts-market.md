<p><p>Emergency ballasts are crucial for ensuring that lighting systems continue to function during power outages or emergencies. There are two main types of emergency ballasts: magnetic and electronic. Magnetic emergency ballasts are older technology that uses heavy transformers to provide power to fluorescent lights. On the other hand, electronic emergency ballasts are more advanced and efficient, using solid-state technology to provide power to LED and fluorescent lights. Both types of emergency ballasts play a critical role in maintaining safety and visibility during emergencies.</p></p>
