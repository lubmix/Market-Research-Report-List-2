<p><p>Mobile Asphalt Plant market can be classified into two main types: Batch Mix Plant and Drum Mix Plant. Batch Mix Plant carries out the mixing process in batches, ensuring high quality and consistency in the final product. On the other hand, Drum Mix Plant involves continuous mixing of asphalt, which is more suitable for large-scale projects requiring a constant supply of hot mix asphalt. Both types of plants offer different advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the construction project.</p></p>
