<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrolyzer market types include traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers are one of the oldest and most commonly used technologies for producing hydrogen through the electrolysis of water. On the other hand, PEM electrolyzers use a solid polymer electrolyte membrane to separate the hydrogen and oxygen gases during the electrolysis process, offering higher efficiency and reliability. Both types of electrolyzers play a crucial role in the growing demand for clean and sustainable hydrogen production.</p></p>
