<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Hair Dryer</li><li>Axial Hair Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld hair dryers are available in two main types based on their internal structure: centrifugal hair dryers and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers use a fan to draw in air and then expel it through a small opening at high speeds to dry hair quickly. On the other hand, axial hair dryers use a propeller-like blade to push air out through a vent to dry hair. Both types of hair dryers are popular in the market and offer different features and benefits for users.</p></p>
