<p><strong>The Universal Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromechanical UTM</li><li>Hydraulic UTM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Universal Testing Machine (UTM) market is segmented into two main types: Electromechanical UTM and Hydraulic UTM. Electromechanical UTM uses an electric motor to apply the load to the specimen, making it a popular choice for testing materials with lower force requirements. On the other hand, Hydraulic UTM utilizes hydraulic pressure to apply the load, making it suitable for testing high-force applications. Both types offer accurate and reliable testing capabilities for a wide range of materials and are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, construction, and research.</p></p>
