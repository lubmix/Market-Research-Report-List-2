<p><p>Ultrasonic cleaning transducers are categorized into two main types: screw hole and non-screw hole. Screw hole transducers are designed with screws for easy installation, making them suitable for applications where frequent assembly and disassembly is required. On the other hand, non-screw hole transducers have a smooth surface and do not require screws for installation, providing a more streamlined and aesthetically pleasing look. Both types offer efficient cleaning capabilities, but the choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
