<p><p>Pu-erh tea is a type of fermented tea that is categorized into two main types: raw Pu-erh tea and cooked Pu-erh tea. Raw Pu-erh tea is made from leaves that have been withered and dried without any additional processing, allowing them to ferment naturally over time. Cooked Pu-erh tea, on the other hand, undergoes an accelerated fermentation process through steaming, compressing, and then fermenting the leaves. Both types of Pu-erh tea have unique flavor profiles and are highly sought after by tea enthusiasts worldwide.</p></p>
