<p><p>Surface Temperature Sensors market can be categorized into various types based on the technology used. Thermocouples are widely used for measuring temperature by generating a voltage proportional to the temperature difference. RTDs offer high accuracy and stability due to their resistance changing with temperature. Infrared sensors detect temperature by measuring the infrared radiation emitted by an object. Other types in the market include thermistors, IC sensors, and bimetallic sensors, each offering unique features and applications for temperature measurement.</p></p>
