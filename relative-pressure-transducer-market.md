<p><p>Relative pressure transducers come in different types based on the technology used for sensing pressure. Strain gauge transducers measure pressure by detecting the strain on a flexible membrane. Diffusion silicon transducers use a silicon diaphragm to measure pressure. Ceramic transducers are made of high-quality ceramic materials for accurate pressure measurement. Other types of relative pressure transducers may include piezoelectric and resonant wire technologies. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial processes.</p></p>
