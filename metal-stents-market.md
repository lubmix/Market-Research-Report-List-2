<p><p>Metal stents are small mesh tubes made of metallic materials such as stainless steel or nitinol, which are used to treat a variety of conditions in the body by providing support to narrowed or blocked blood vessels or other tubular structures. They are commonly used in the treatment of conditions such as coronary artery disease, gastrointestinal disorders, and urological obstructions.</p><p>The Metal Stents Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.20% during the forecast period. The key factors driving the market growth include the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases such as cardiovascular diseases, cancer, and gastrointestinal disorders, advancements in stent technology leading to improved patient outcomes, and rising demand for minimally invasive procedures. Additionally, the growing aging population worldwide, along with the increasing adoption of metal stents due to their durability and efficacy, is expected to further boost market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Metal Stents Market include the development of bioabsorbable stents, which are gradually absorbed by the body over time, reducing the risk of long-term complications. Additionally, the increasing focus on personalized medicine and the use of drug-eluting stents to prevent restenosis after stent placement are expected to drive market growth in the coming years.</p></p>
