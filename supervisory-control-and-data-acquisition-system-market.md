<p><p>Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition System (SCADA) market is segmented into three main types: Programmable Logic Control (PLC), Human Machine Interface (HMI), and Remote Terminal Unit (RTU). PLC allows for direct control over machinery, HMI provides a visual interface for operators to monitor and control processes, and RTU collects data from sensors and equipment in the field. Each market type plays a crucial role in SCADA systems by facilitating efficient and effective control and monitoring of various industrial processes.</p></p>
