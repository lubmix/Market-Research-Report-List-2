<p><p>An antistatic device is a tool used to reduce or eliminate static electricity in a specific area. Antistatic devices work by either neutralizing the static charge or grounding it to prevent sparking or electric shock.</p><p>The global antistatic device market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.4% during the forecast period.
