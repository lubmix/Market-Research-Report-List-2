<p><p>Vascular access market types include CVC Catheters, Implantable Ports, Dialysis Catheters, and PICC Catheters. Central Venous Catheters (CVC) are used for long-term administration of medication or fluids. Implantable Ports provide secure access for frequent treatments like chemotherapy. Dialysis Catheters are used for hemodialysis to filter blood in patients with kidney failure. Peripherally Inserted Central Catheters (PICC) are long, thin tubes inserted in a vein for long-term medication administration. These vascular access devices play crucial roles in medical treatment and care.</p></p>
