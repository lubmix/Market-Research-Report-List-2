<p><p>Kitchen scales market can be categorized into two types: mechanical scales and digital scales. Mechanical scales have a traditional design and operate using springs and levers to measure weight. They are often more affordable but may be less precise than digital scales. Digital scales, on the other hand, use electronic sensors and display weight readings digitally. They are known for their accuracy and ease of use. Both types of scales are widely available in the market to suit different preferences and needs.</p></p>
