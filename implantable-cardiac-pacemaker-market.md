<p><p>The implantable cardiac pacemaker market includes various types of devices such as pacemakers, implantable cardioverter defibrillators (ICDs), and biventricular ICDs (Bi-V ICDs). Pacemakers are designed to regulate the heart's electrical activity, while ICDs are used to prevent sudden cardiac arrest by delivering an electrical shock when necessary. Bi-V ICDs are specialized devices that provide synchronized pacing to both sides of the heart. These devices are essential in the treatment of various arrhythmias and heart conditions, driving growth in the implantable cardiac pacemaker market.</p></p>
