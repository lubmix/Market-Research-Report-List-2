<p><p>Piezo Inkjet Printhead market can be broadly classified into two types based on the technology used - MEMS Inkjet Printhead and Non-MEMS Inkjet Printhead. MEMS Inkjet Printheads use micro-electromechanical system technology to create precise droplets, offering high-resolution printing capabilities. On the other hand, Non-MEMS Inkjet Printheads rely on traditional piezoelectric elements to facilitate ink ejection. Both types of printheads cater to different market segments and offer unique advantages in terms of performance, cost, and compatibility with various printing applications.</p></p>
