<p><p>Ambulatory infusion pumps are categorized into two main types - mechanics pumps and electronic pumps. Mechanics pumps are operated manually by the user, providing a more basic and simple method of delivering medication. On the other hand, electronic pumps are automated devices that control the rate and timing of medication administration, offering precision and convenience. Both types of pumps are used in ambulatory settings to deliver various medications such as antibiotics, pain management drugs, and chemotherapy.</p></p>
