<p><p>Resistance welding machines are categorized into three types based on their level of automation - Manual, Semi-Automatic, and Automatic. Manual resistance welding machines require operators to manually control the welding process, making them suitable for small-scale operations. Semi-Automatic machines partially automate the process, reducing the need for manual intervention. Automatic resistance welding machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human involvement and increasing efficiency in high-volume production environments. Each type offers varying levels of control and efficiency to cater to different welding requirements.</p></p>
