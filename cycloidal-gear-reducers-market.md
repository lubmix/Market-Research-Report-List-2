<p><p>Cycloidal gear reducers are available in two main types: horizontal-type and vertical-type. Horizontal-type reducers are commonly used in industries where space is limited or there are strict height restrictions. They are designed to be mounted horizontally and are ideal for applications where compactness is essential. In contrast, vertical-type reducers are designed to be mounted vertically, making them suitable for applications where the input and output shafts are perpendicular. Both types offer efficient power transmission and torque amplification, meeting the needs of various industrial applications.</p></p>
