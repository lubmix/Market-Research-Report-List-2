<p><p>Composite brake shoes are available in two main types: synthetic resin base and rubber base. Synthetic resin base brake shoes are made from materials like phenolic resins, which offer high durability and heat resistance. They are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles and industrial machinery. On the other hand, rubber base brake shoes are made from a combination of rubber and other materials, providing flexibility and good grip on different surfaces. They are often used in light vehicles and motorcycles for efficient braking performance.</p></p>
