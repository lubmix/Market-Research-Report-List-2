<p><p>Fiberglass fans come in two main market types - belt drive and direct drive. Belt drive fans are typically used in applications that require higher static pressure and airflow, such as industrial settings. They have a belt-driven motor that allows for adjustable speed settings. Direct drive fans, on the other hand, have the motor directly connected to the fan blades, making them more efficient and easier to maintain. They are commonly used in commercial and residential settings where noise levels and energy efficiency are important factors.</p></p>
