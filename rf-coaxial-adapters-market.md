<p><p>RF Coaxial Adapters serve to connect different types of RF cables or devices and come in two main types: In Series and Between Series. In Series RF Coaxial Adapters are used to connect two cables with the same type of connectors, such as SMA to SMA. Between Series RF Coaxial Adapters are used to connect cables with different types of connectors, like SMA to N-Type. Both types of adapters play a crucial role in maintaining signal integrity and ensuring seamless connections in RF systems.</p></p>
