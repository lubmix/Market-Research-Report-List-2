<p><p>A water heater expansion tank is a small tank that is installed in a water heating system to help absorb excess pressure caused by the thermal expansion of water. As water heats up, it expands and can cause fluctuations in pressure within the system. The expansion tank provides a space for the expanded water to go, preventing pressure buildup and potential damage to the water heater and plumbing system.</p><p>The Water Heater Expansion Tank Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period.
