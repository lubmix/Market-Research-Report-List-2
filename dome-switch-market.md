<p><strong>The Dome Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Mount</li><li>Through Hole Mounting</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dome switch market includes three main types: SMD mount, through hole mounting, and others. SMD mount dome switches are soldered directly onto the surface of a PCB, helping to save space and reduce assembly time. Through hole mounting dome switches require holes to be drilled in the PCB for installation. The "others" category includes dome switches that do not fall into the SMD or through hole mounting categories, such as snap dome switches. Each type has its own benefits and applications within the market.</p></p>
