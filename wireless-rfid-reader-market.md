<p><p>Wireless RFID readers are available in two main market types: fixed/wall-mounted and portable. Fixed RFID readers are typically mounted in a stationary location such as doorways or ceilings, providing continuous monitoring of tagged items or people passing through their range. On the other hand, portable RFID readers are handheld devices that can be carried around to scan tags in various locations. Fixed readers are ideal for high-volume, automated tracking systems, while portable readers offer the flexibility for on-the-go scanning and inventory management.</p></p>
