<p><p>The eye tracking hardware market can be categorized into two main types: screen eye trackers and wearable eye trackers. Screen eye trackers are devices that are mounted on or integrated into a computer monitor or screen to track the user's eye movements and gaze patterns. On the other hand, wearable eye trackers are portable devices that can be worn on the user's head or attached to glasses to track eye movements in real-time. Both types of eye trackers offer valuable insights for various applications such as research, gaming, and assistive technologies.</p></p>
