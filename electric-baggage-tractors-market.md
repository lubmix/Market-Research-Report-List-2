<p><p>Electric baggage tractors can be categorized into two types based on the type of battery used: Lead-Acid Battery and Li-Ion Battery markets. Lead-Acid Battery tractors use traditional lead-acid batteries, which are cost-effective but heavier and require more maintenance. In contrast, Li-Ion Battery tractors utilize lithium-ion batteries, which are lighter, have a longer lifespan, and require less maintenance. Li-Ion Battery tractors are gaining popularity due to their superior performance and efficiency, although they come at a higher initial cost.</p></p>
