<p><ul><li>Pusher Seals</li><li>Non-pusher Seals</li><li>Cartridge Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical pump seals market consists of different types such as pusher seals, non-pusher seals, and cartridge seals. Pusher seals are designed with an internal spring mechanism that pushes the seal face against the rotating shaft. Non-pusher seals do not have this mechanism and rely on other methods to maintain contact with the shaft. Cartridge seals are pre-assembled units that are easy to install and replace. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for specific applications in various industries.</p></p>
