<p><p>Gas barbecues come in two main types: liquid propane (LP) barbecues and natural gas (NG) barbecues. LP barbecues are fueled by portable propane tanks, making them convenient for outdoor use. NG barbecues are connected directly to a natural gas line, eliminating the need for refilling tanks but limiting their portability. LP barbecues are popular for their ease of use and mobility, while NG barbecues are favored for their convenience and cost-effectiveness in the long run. Both types offer reliable heat sources for outdoor cooking.</p></p>
