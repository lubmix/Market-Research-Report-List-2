<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Transformers for Switching Power Supplies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-excited</li><li>Double-excited</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transformers for switching power supplies can be categorized into two main types based on their excitation method: single-excited and double-excited transformers. </p><p>Single-excited transformers have only one winding that is excited by the input voltage, while double-excited transformers have two windings that are excited separately by different voltages. </p><p>Single-excited transformers are commonly used in simpler power supply applications, while double-excited transformers are preferred for more complex power supply designs requiring precise regulation and control of multiple output voltages. Both types play a crucial role in efficiently converting and regulating power in electronic devices.</p></p>
