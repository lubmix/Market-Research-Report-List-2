<p><p>The pen multimeter market is primarily divided into two types: digital multimeters and pointer multimeters. Digital multimeters use a digital display to provide precise measurements of voltage, current, and resistance. They offer high accuracy and are commonly used for professional and advanced applications. Pointer multimeters, on the other hand, have a traditional analog display with a moving pointer to indicate measurements. They are simpler to use and are often preferred for basic testing and troubleshooting tasks. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
