<p><strong>The Aluminium Conductor Steel-reinforced Cable (ACSR) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ACSR – Aluminum Conductor Steel-Reinforced</li><li>ACSR/AW – Aluminum Conductor Aluminum-Clad Steel-Reinforced</li><li>ACSR/TW – Trapezoidal Aluminum Conductor Steel-Reinforced</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ACSR (Aluminum Conductor Steel-Reinforced) cables are commonly used in overhead power transmission lines due to their high tensile strength and conductivity. ACSR/AW (Aluminum Conductor Aluminum-Clad Steel-Reinforced) cables have a layer of aluminum cladding over the steel core for improved corrosion resistance. ACSR/TW (Trapezoidal Aluminum Conductor Steel-Reinforced) cables have a trapezoidal-shaped aluminum conductor for increased strength and reduced sag. Each type of ACSR cable offers unique advantages for different applications within the power transmission industry.</p></p>
