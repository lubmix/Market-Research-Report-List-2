<p><p>The Electrostatic Sensor Market can be classified into two main types: Hand-Held and In-Line. Hand-Held sensors are portable devices that can be carried around for on-the-go measurements, making them convenient for quick and easy testing. On the other hand, In-Line sensors are stationary devices that are integrated into manufacturing processes to provide continuous monitoring and control of electrostatic charges. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in detecting static electricity and ensuring the safety and efficiency of various industrial operations.</p></p>
