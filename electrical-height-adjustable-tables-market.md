<p><p>Electric height adjustable tables come in two main market types: single motor and dual motor. Single motor tables have one motor that controls the height adjustment of the table, making them a more cost-effective option for basic height adjustment needs. On the other hand, dual motor tables have two motors, providing more stability and capacity for heavier loads. Dual motor tables are typically more expensive but offer smoother and faster height adjustments, making them a better choice for more demanding working environments.</p></p>
