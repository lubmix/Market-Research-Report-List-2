<p><p>Absorbable hemostats are medical devices used to control bleeding during surgical procedures. There are several types of absorbable hemostats available in the market, including hemostatic gauze, styptic powder, and gelatin sponge. Hemostatic gauze is made of natural or synthetic fibers that help promote clotting and stop bleeding. Styptic powder contains ingredients that promote blood clotting when applied to a wound. Gelatin sponge is a porous material that absorbs blood and promotes clotting at the site of bleeding. Each type of absorbable hemostat has specific applications in different surgical settings.</p></p>
