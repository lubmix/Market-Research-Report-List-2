<p><p>Online moisture analyzers are used in various industries to measure the moisture content of materials. Two common types of analyzers are Near Infrared Resonance (NIR) and Radio Frequency (RF). NIR analyzers use light waves to analyze the composition of materials, while RF analyzers measure the dielectric properties of materials. Both types offer accurate and reliable moisture analysis, with NIR analyzers being more suitable for solid materials and RF analyzers for bulk materials like grain and chemicals. These analyzers play a crucial role in ensuring product quality and process efficiency.</p></p>
