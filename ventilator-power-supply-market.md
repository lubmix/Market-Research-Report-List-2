<p><p>Ventilator power supply market types include AC/DC power supply, DC/DC power supply, and internal backup batteries. AC/DC power supplies are used to convert alternating current into direct current for ventilators, while DC/DC power supplies are used to convert one level of DC voltage to another. Internal backup batteries provide a backup power source for ventilators in case of power outages or failures. These different market types cater to various power supply needs for ventilator operation, ensuring continuous and reliable functioning.</p></p>
