<p><p>Loading dock equipment is classified into three main types: mechanical, hydraulic, and others. Mechanical equipment, such as dock levelers and lifts, rely on manual or motorized mechanisms to assist in loading and unloading goods. Hydraulic equipment, like dock lifts and scissor lifts, use hydraulic power to raise or lower platforms for easy access to trucks and trailers. The "others" category includes a variety of specialized equipment such as air-powered lifts or specialty dock levelers. Each type offers unique features and benefits to improve efficiency and safety in the loading dock environment.</p></p>
