<p><p>Brake fluid is classified into different types based on their chemical compositions and performance capabilities. DOT 3 and DOT 4 are glycol-based fluids suitable for most vehicles, while DOT 5 is silicone-based and is commonly used in high-performance or classic cars due to its higher boiling point. DOT 5.1 is a glycol-based fluid with similar properties to DOT 3 and DOT 4 but with a higher boiling point. Each type of brake fluid is designed to meet specific requirements and provide optimal braking performance under various driving conditions.</p></p>
