<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Magnetic Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Magnetic Bearing</li><li>Passive Magnetic Bearing</li><li>Hybrid Magnetic Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Magnetic Bearings use electromagnets controlled by a computer to suspend the rotor in place, allowing for precise control and maintenance of position. Passive Magnetic Bearings use permanent magnets to create a magnetic field that supports the rotor without the need for external power. Hybrid Magnetic Bearings combine aspects of both active and passive systems to achieve a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. Each type of magnetic bearing offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications based on the required level of control and complexity.</p></p>
