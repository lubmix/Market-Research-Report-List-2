<p><p>Soy sauce comes in two main types: brewed and blended. Brewed soy sauce is traditionally made by fermenting soybeans, wheat, salt, and water for several months to develop its deep umami flavor. On the other hand, blended soy sauce is made by mixing various ingredients together to achieve a consistent taste. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with brewed soy sauce being favored for its complex flavors and artisanal production, while blended soy sauce offers a more standardized taste profile.</p></p>
