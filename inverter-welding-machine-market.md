<p><p>Inverter welding machines come in various types, including MMA (manual metal arc), MIG/MAG (metal inert gas/metal active gas), TIG (tungsten inert gas), and Others. MMA machines are commonly used for stick welding, MIG/MAG for wire feeding welding, and TIG for high-quality welding of thin materials. The 'Others' category includes specialized welding processes like plasma arc welding or submerged arc welding. Each type of inverter welding machine offers unique features and benefits tailored to different welding applications.</p></p>
