<p><p>A Vibrating Rod Level Switch is a liquid level sensor used in industrial applications to detect the presence or absence of liquid in a tank or vessel. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for efficient and reliable level sensing solutions in industries such as oil & gas, chemical, food & beverage, and pharmaceuticals.
